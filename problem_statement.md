Problem Statement
The Problem:
Ensuring the safety of workers on construction sites is a significant challenge. Manual monitoring for Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is often difficult due to the large scale of sites and the high number of workers. Human error and fatigue can lead to missed safety checks, which can have life-threatening consequences. 🏗️

The Solution:
This project uses an automated AI system based on the YOLOv8 model to provide a "second set of eyes" 👁️ on construction sites. The goal is to detect PPE in real-time and alert safety officers if any worker is missing their gear, thus overcoming the limitations of manual monitoring and enhancing overall site safety. 🛡️
